Instructions

The organisers of PubRun (Lisa plus leadership team) will be able to set up new runners on the system, view all registered runners, generate QR codes and view which runners have indicated they are coming along to a run. Anonymous users (i.e. pubrunners themselves) will be able to sign up for a run and view their QR codes.

When a runner comes along to a run, they will show one of the organisers their QR code. The organiser will already have logged in on their phone / tablet to this site, and when they scan the QR code, it automatically updates the register of runners to indicate that the person has "arrived", while also showing the list of runners expected along to PubRun that day.
